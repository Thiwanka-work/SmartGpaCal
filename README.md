## 🚀 Overview

**SmartGPA** is a modern, privacy-focused academic management system designed specifically for university students. Operating entirely in your browser using local storage, SmartGPA tracks your GPA, CGPA, and semester progress while using smart analytics to predict exactly what grades you need to achieve your academic goals.

Featuring a beautiful, responsive, glass-morphism interface, it is the perfect companion for staying on top of your university journey.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📊 Academic Dashboard
- **Live CGPA & GPA Statistics:** View your current standing instantly.
- **Credit Completion Tracking:** Keep an eye on how close you are to graduation.
- **Trend Indicators:** See if your performance is trending up or down.

### 🔮 Advanced Prediction Engine (NEW!)
- **Target Selection:** Aim for First Class, Second Upper, or customize your goal.
- **Per-Semester Targets:** The engine mathematically determines the exact GPA you need for each upcoming semester.
- **Target Achieved Highlights:** Cards turn green and show a celebratory badge when you've hit the target GPA for a recorded semester!
- **Feasibility Check:** Immediately know if your goal is mathematically impossible with your remaining credits.

### ➕ Smart Semester Management
- **Skip Semesters:** Easily skip internship or leave semesters where no GPA was recorded, while still tracking credits.
- **Quick Previews:** See how a new semester will impact your overall CGPA before you even add it.

### 💡 Interactive GPA Calculator
- **Course-by-Course Math:** Calculate your semester GPA by entering course credits and grades.
- **Grade Upgrade Hints:** Enter a grade of C- or below, and SmartGPA instantly suggests an upgrade to a 'C', showing exactly how it would improve your semester GPA and overall CGPA!
- **Auto-Save:** Seamlessly port your calculated GPA directly into your semester record.

### 📈 Performance Analytics
- **Visual Trends:** Line charts mapping your GPA progression over time (powered by Chart.js).
- **Credit Doughnuts:** Visual breakdown of completed vs. remaining credits.
- **Extremes Detection:** Automatically identifies your best and most challenging semesters.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built for maximum performance, security (100% local), and aesthetics:

- **HTML5:** Semantic and structured.
- **CSS3:** Advanced animations, glass-morphism UI, custom CSS variables, and full responsive design.
- **Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+):** Zero-dependency state management using `localStorage`.
- **Chart.js:** For rendering crisp, interactive data visualizations.
- **Lucide Icons:** Premium, modern SVG icons for a sleek aesthetic.
- **Google Fonts:** Utilizing 'Inter' for highly legible typography.
